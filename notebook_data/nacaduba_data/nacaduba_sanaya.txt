Nacaduba sanaya elioti (The Jewelled 4-line Blue) belongs to the pavana group (the 4-line Blues) of the genus Nacaduba.

Underside Identification Keys

Group Characteristic: On the underside of the forewing, it lacks a pair of pale subbasal lines in the cell.

Forewing Markings: The postdiscal band is curved in towards the base and is often slightly dislocated at vein 6.

Hindwing Markings: The inner submarginal and outer postdiscal striae are separated by at least half the width of the postdiscal band.

Striae Appearance: The underside markings consist of narrow, well-defined white or whitish striae.

Wing Length and Size

Forewing Length: Measures between 13.0 mm and 14.5 mm.

Tail Information

Tail: This species possesses a filamentous tail at vein 2 of the hindwing.
